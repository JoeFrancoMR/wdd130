History

Founded in 2024 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts, RiverRush Rafting Company has been dedicated to providing exhilarating rafting experiences with a focus on safety, environmental stewardship, and exceptional customer service. Starting with a single raft and a passion for adventure, RiverRush quickly earned a reputation for excellence, offering a variety of trips for adventurers of all levels. Today, RiverRush remains a leader in outdoor adventure tourism, inspiring countless individuals to discover the thrill of whitewater rafting in some of the most stunning natural settings. Join us at RiverRush and experience the excitement of whitewater rafting with a company committed to adventure, safety, and unforgettable memories.
